The Shattering

After the Elden Ring was broken by someone—or something—the Greater Will’s grace vanished from the Lands Between. The children of Queen Marika, each claiming a Great Rune, waged endless war. This epoch is known as The Shattering.

Outer Gods

Beyond the Lands Between exist cosmic powers that pull the strings of fate.

  • The Greater Will – sent the Elden Beast, created the Golden Order.
  • Frenzied Flame – seeks to melt all distinctions; opposes individuality.
  • Formless Mother – source of Mohg’s bloodflame; crimson ooze.
  • God of Rot – sealed by the blind swordsman; Malenia’s third bloom risks unleashing it.

Queen Marika & Radagon

Marika is both vessel and prisoner of the Elden Ring. Radagon, her male half, tried to repair the Ring yet is doomed to fracture it further. Their duality is symbolized by the statue in Leyndell that shifts between forms when struck by Law of Regression.

The Tarnished

Exiles who lost the grace of gold long ago. Called back from the Badlands after the Shattering, guided by grace’s light to become Elden Lord. You are one of them—neither demigod nor mere mortal, but a contender for the throne.

Lands Between – Major Regions

  • Limgrave – gentle golden plains, Stormgate fortress.
  • Liurnia – misty lake studded with glintstone academies.
  • Caelid – scarlet rot wasteland where Radahn halted the stars.
  • Altus Plateau – golden capital Leyndell, seat of the Erdtree.
  • Mountaintops of the Giants – frozen ruins of the fire giants.
  • Haligtree – secret refuge grown by Malenia & Miquella.
  • Crucible & Deeproot – primordial life-blood beneath the capital.
